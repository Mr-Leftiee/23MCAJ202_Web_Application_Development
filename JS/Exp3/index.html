Mumbai Indians (MI) is a professional T20 franchise cricket team based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, that competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and is owned by Reliance Industries. They have won the IPL trophy five times, making them the most successful team in the league, and have a strong core of players including Rohit Sharma, Hardik Pandya, Suryakumar Yadav, and Jasprit Bumrah.
